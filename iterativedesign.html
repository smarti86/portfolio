Problem:

Create a streamlined purchasing flow for CUR8, a carbon removal brokerage aiming to remove 1 billion tonnes of carbon annually.

The existing over-the-hone portfolio creation process slowed down customer acquisition. We aimed to simplify this process,ensuring users could purchase carbon removal portfolios straight from the CUR8 website

Research:

We conducted a competitive analysis. After dicussing about CUR8's goals, we identified:

  • Lune's journey to curate carbon credit portfolios as intuative
  • Frontier's portfolio breakdown and projects details as a helpful feature

Our peers and TAs also gave us feedback on our low-fidelity mockup. With their help, we added a feature for stakeholder collaboration for an intuitive, frictionless purchasing flow.

Process Details:

Our design iterations began with independent sketches, resulting in eight distinct flows. Here are 2 examples:

For our first iteration wireframing:
We consolidated our ideas, focusing on 4 essential pages: portfolio summary, cart, and confirmation.

For our last iteration wireframing: Focusing solely on payment pages that I developed, users review their portfolio and proceed to the purchase request page. If stakeholders weren't previously invited, a banner alert prompts users to do so. Upon submission, users receive a confirmation message to return to their portfolio or exit.

Solution:

Our final design comprised a cohesive purchasing flow tailored to CUR8's requirements.

The flow seamlessly guided users through project selection, stakeholder collaboration, and purchase request submission. Features like project breakdowns, stakeholder feedback, and a receipt-like review page enhanced user understanding and engagement. By aligning with CUR8's brand identity and providing a user-friendly experience, our solution aimed to streamline the carbon removal portfolio purchasing process.

Learnings:

  • Adapting our designs based on peer feedback and research findings
  • Working for CUR8 and having their needs guide our project direction
  • Industry communications and Zoom etiquette

Role:

As part of a multidisciplinary team including Angie Osei-Ampadu, Sandy Martinez, and Ashley Cai, I played a collaborative role in designing and refining CUR8's purchasing flow.
