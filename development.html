Seamless Shopping:
Sandy's Tanks & Tees

Individual Project for the Brown University CSCI 1300 Course

Conducted a competitive analysis, Made low fidelity mockup, Created the webpage

Figma, React, HTML, CSS

Problem:

Shopping for t-shirts and tees online can be a daunting task due to the lack of intuitive filtering and sorting options. Users often find themselves lost in a sea of options, leading to frustration and inefficiency in their shopping journey. Recognizing this pain point, I set out to create Sandy's Tanks & Tees, a platform designed to simplify the online shopping experience.

Research:

To craft an effective website, I conducted an in-depth competitive analysis of industry giants like Zara, Redbubble, and Not On The High Street. This research provided invaluable insights into user preferences regarding filtering, sorting, and aggregation features. Understanding the needs of my target audience, individuals seeking quality t-shirts and tees online, was pivotal in shaping my approach.

Process Details:

  • I began by considering the iterative approach guided by insights from research and user-centric principles.
    Decision: After finding the high quality tanks & tees, I began sketching the general layout of the website with the tranditional grid layout.

  • Then, I explored various filtering and sorting options, considering complexity and usability tradeoffs.
    Decision: The filtering and sorting options are placed at the top of the webpage, the intuative place for the user

  • Next, I strategically prioritized essential features such as size, color, and price filtering. Decision: I included the basic filtering options to remain close to my mission: simplify the online shopping experience

  • Finally, I created a webpage with minimal visual clutter and that maintained a minimalist aesthetic. Below are screencaptures of Sandy's Tanks & Tees

Solution:

Sandy's Tanks & Tees offers a refined and user-friendly marketplace for online shopping. Inspired by my research findings, I implemented intuitive filtering and sorting mechanisms that empower users to find their perfect t-shirt or tee effortlessly. The dynamic cart feature ensures a seamless transition from browsing to cart, enhancing the overall user experience.


To check out, visit Sandy's Tanks & Tees !

Design Insights:

  • Gained valuable insights into the importance of prioritizing user needs and maintaining simplicity in design.
  • Learned to balance functionality with visual aesthetics, making informed decisions to optimize usability.
  • Importance of conventions: my cart is at the bottom of the webpage, not hidden behind an icon or in a sidebar. In a future iteration, I would place the cart in a place where the user expects it, such as the sidebar.
