Problem:

The problem I set out to solve was the frustrating experience users often face when shopping for t-shirts and tees online. Many existing websites lack intuitive filtering and sorting options, leading to a cluttered and inefficient shopping journey. Recognizing the need for a solution that prioritizes simplicity and functionality, I created Sandy's Tanks and Tees, a website that would streamline the online shopping experience for users.

Research:

My research involved conducting a thorough competitive analysis of Zara, Redbubble, and Not On The High Street. Through this research and a competitive comparison, I gained valuable insights into the filtering, sorting, and aggregation features that resonated with users. Here is a summary on the factors I observed:

My target audience comprised individuals seeking quality t-shirts and tees online, who valued ease of navigation and personalized shopping experiences.

Process Details:

  • Iterative approach guided by insights from research and user-centric principles.
  • Exploration of various filtering and sorting options, considering complexity and usability tradeoffs.
  • Strategic prioritization of essential features such as size, color, and price filtering.
  • Minimization of visual clutter and maintenance of a minimalist aesthetic.

Solution:

My solution, Sandy's T-Shirts and Tees, showcases a refined and user-friendly marketplace for online shopping. By implementing intuitive filtering and sorting mechanisms inspired by my research, I created an environment where users can effortlessly find their perfect t-shirt or tee. The inclusion of a dynamic cart feature ensures a seamless transition from browsing to purchasing, enhancing the overall user experience.

Design Insights:

  • Gained valuable insights into the importance of prioritizing user needs and maintaining simplicity in design.
  • Learned to balance functionality with visual aesthetics, making informed decisions to optimize usability.
  • Highlighted the significance of iterative design and the value of adapting to constraints while remaining focused on delivering a superior user experience.

Role:

I played the role of both designer and programmer by spearheading the development of Sandy's T-Shirts and Tees.
